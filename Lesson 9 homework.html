Application for Transfer of Registration

An application can be completed online at service.nsw.gov.au/transaction/transfer-vehicle-registration or in person (not mail) at a service centre within 14 days of purchasing/acquiring a vehicle or a late application fee of at least $121 will be charged. Costs shown are correct at the time of printing, but can be subject to change without notice. Check the last page of this form for a guide to applying to transfer registration. Note: To register a vehicle in your name you must be at least 16 years of age for a light vehicle (up to 4.5 tonnes GVM) and at least 18 years of age for a heavy vehicle (over 4.5 tonnes GVM). To complete an application online you must have an online services account and the notice of disposal must be lodged by the seller and recorded by Service NSW.
